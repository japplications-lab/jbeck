What is jMouse?

jMouse is a desktop utility designed to prevent your workstation from going idle by simulating subtle mouse movements and virtual key presses at regular intervals. It keeps your system awake, prevents screensavers, and maintains your presence status active in communication tools like Microsoft Teams.

Intended for legitimate use cases like long downloads, remote sessions, monitoring dashboards, or presentations.
